Easier on the eyes for late-night entries.

Shows initials (e.g. "A.W.") on screen instead of full names. PDF statements still show full names.

🔒 App Lock (Password)

Require a password to open this app on this device. Leave blank and save to remove the lock.

⚠️ This is a basic device-level lock, not encryption. Don't forget the password — there's no recovery option, you'd need to clear browser data to reset.

👆 Fingerprint / Face Unlock

Use your device's fingerprint or face unlock instead of typing a password.

⚠️ Only works when this app is opened over HTTPS (e.g. your NitiForge site) — not when opened as a local file. Falls back to your password automatically if unavailable.

🛡️ Require Auth to Edit/Delete

Ask for your password or fingerprint every time before editing or deleting any record — not just when opening the app.
